About Me


Hello! I'm Enzo Seurre, an interaction designer mainly focused on games and interactive experiences, with a strong inclination toward social issues. I enjoy creating playful yet meaningful experiences that question our relationship with technology and how we can use it without letting it use us. But sometimes, I also like to experiment things just for fun! In my practice, I'm mostly interested by doing games and 3D. You can explore my projects by clicking on their names in the menu!

You can also see my socials by clicking the icons below to further see my projects, try some of my games or to get in touch!
